MANUFACTURING

Growing competition and globalized supply networks is driving the Manufacturing industry. With rapidly changing customer expectations and constant technological change, there is a need for quick product development and innovation. Increasing usage of embedded software in the product enables them to be more intelligent bringing with it the challenge of huge amount of equipment data that needs be processed and analyzed to produce meaningful insights.

We, at Ceone, understand the Manufacturing domain and its challenges including demand fluctuations, regulatory issues, capacity underutilization and margin pressure. Our experts ensure that we provide relevant point solutions to address the identified challenges. We focus in providing solutions to address supply chain bottlenecks, ensuring sustainable growth, addressing regulatory compliance requirements.


We provide our customized solutions addressing the specific manufacturing industry segments. Some of the industry segments where we can address the client’s challenges include:

  • Water Utility Services
  • Electric Utility Services
  • Waste and Recycling Services
  • Optimizing SAP for Utilities
  • Integration of geospatial systems
  • Internet of Things (IoT) devices
  • Reliability and security
