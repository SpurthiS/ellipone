“FEED services to select best design options and
error free engineering”.

Capturing best design solutions to provide accurate 3D modelling and improve decision making


Our FEED service provides comprehensive coverage of all aspects and disciplines of the Front End Engineering Design application, including intelligent models in SP3D, PDS, CADWorx, PDMS and Plant3D along with specifications and catalogues, 2D Drawings, ISOs, P&ID verification, etc.

We use a combination of proprietary in-built automation, specialized expertise, and a well-organised, scalable technical team to set industry benchmarks for quality and schedule, while remaining highly cost competitive with alternatives.

We provide Modelling services for Front End Engineering & Design for Oil & Gas, Petrochemicals, Refineries, Power & other process industries.
