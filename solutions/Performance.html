Managing assets that prioritizes business objectives

Ability to have optimal Asset Reliability, Availability and Performance is essential


A diverse set of complex assets are regularly used in the Process Industry. Managing these assets by continuously tracking performance health, even remotely, has been a constant challenge. The key stakeholders do understand that such real-time health and performance insights can be used to influence decisions and actions that will drive efficiency and improve competitive advantage.

Reactive and break-fix maintenance of critical production assets often leads to high physical and financial risk. The preventive maintenance approach using the Asset Performance Management, can be used to generate meaningful metrics using the continuous inputs of monitored asset performance data. Thus APM and its functionalities influences - supply, operations and almost all other business functions that finally translates into business value for the end customer.

Ceone’s solutions aim at optimizing the performance of the assets


Ceone, with its in depth understanding on the functionalities of various assets within the process industry, has built solutions for connecting the disparate data sources and providing actionable insights.

Our APM solutions have the capabilities of data capture, integration, visualization and analytics integrated together, with an aim to improve the reliability and availability of physical assets.The solution also aims at helping in better inspection management and maintenance, optimizing repair and operations inventories, and prioritizing replacement versus repair decisions.


Features of our APM Solutions

REMOTE MONITORING: Ability to remotely monitor critical assets and operations in a near.

PREDICTIVE MAINTENANCE: Conduct proactive maintenance using IOT & AI technology. Reduce unplanned downtime and increase availability and reliability.

HIRERARCHIAL VIEW: View the related assets hierarchically and get a better picture of the root cause analysis.

OPTIMISED WORKFLOW: Ability to remotely monitor critical assets and operations in a near real-time mode.

CLOUD BASED: Cloud adapters simplify support of different protocols without any burden on sensor or gateway providers.

PLUG -N- PLAY: Plug-and-Play solution that supports multi-vendor protocols and easily migrates to modern digital infrastructure.

Benefits

  • Optimize Productivity: Improve workforce productivity by prioritizing maintenance based on criticality and cost rather than schedule-based maintenance practices.
  • Lower total cost of ownership: Reduce TCO delivered via a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) model, providing flexibility and access to business insights.
  • Faster time-to-value: A standardized way to connect machines, data, and people with a consistent interface for superior user experience, dynamic scalability, and extendibility to grow functionality as business needs evolve.
  • Optimize asset health: Transform the approach by driving actions based on the current health of assets.
  • Reduces often unnecessary routine preventive maintenance which also introduces risk and decreases availability.
