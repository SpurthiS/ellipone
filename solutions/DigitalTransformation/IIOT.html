IIOT PLATFORM:

Eliminating silos across sensor networks, SCADA systems,
and M2M applications to enable improving machine
productivity and implementing digital services

Most of the process-oriented Industries and Building Automation have been using Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) applications for better asset management, Remote monitoring, SCM, Predictive maintenance, operational efficiency and informed decision making.

Ceone with its domain and technology expertise, offers a cloud service or as software that can be deployed in an enterprise private cloud. It allows to easily manage, scale, and apply sensor data to make better business decisions and streamline operations.

Our framework supports innovative IoT use cases by breaking silos across isolated sensor networks, SCADA systems, and machine-to-machine applications. It allows one to rapidly design applications for energy management, ventilation control, workflow automation, surveillance, asset tracking, and many more. In general, customers save 20-35% of energy and operational costs while creating efficient processes.

POWERFUL MULTI-VENDOR, MULTI-PROTOCOL SOLUTION

Plug-and-Play: Unique solution that supports for 150+ protocols to connect any device, integrate with partner gateways and allows Real-Time visualization and workflow automation.

Flexible IoT Abstraction: Use pre-built or develop custom abstractions for policy management including multi-vendor interoperability with investment protection, Combining real or simulated data from anywhere and Integration of location and video capabilities for powerful context for decision-making.

Workflow Automation: Configure policies centrally and enforce them at the edge with Autonomous, low-latency edge gateways, Policy enforcement and traffic engineering and Plug-ins for Email, SMS, and custom actions.

Service Integration: Cloud adapters for interoperability and data Translations with REST APIs for secure partner and third-party Integration.
