Digital Twin for efficient Operations and Maintenance


In principle, a digital twin is a virtual representation of the key attributes of a physical object or process that can be tracked and updated along the lifecycle to achieve design and operational efficiencies.

Ceone, in collaboration with market leaders, provide the digital twin platform-based solutions (for Discrete and Composite level).

We do understand that many of the industry players are at various stages of digital maturity and our solutions try to address the requirements based on existing maturity across the asset life cycle.

A TECHNOLOGICAL LEAP TO ADDRESS THE CURRENT CHALLENGES IN ENERGY SECTOR

Industries need to take advantage of digital tin, both in ensuring efficient and safe ongoing operations and in designing new techniques and facilities.

In the operations, our solution will help in making informed-decision around optimizing production and maintenance by assessing how actions or events affect a virtual model of an asset.

In the current scenario, this technology is being undertaken in the design of platforms and similar installations, with data on both existing and planned installations being constantly fed into the models.

Digital Twin for Asset Lifecycle Management


Our solution synchronises CAD, BIM, GIS, Photos, LIDAR scans, business system data, IoT data feeds, etc. into the model to develop following functional D-Twins.

Project and Construction 7D D-TWIN

  • 3D Model
  • Cost
  • Schedule
  • Safety Management
  • Asset Performance Management

    • Operate and Maintain 8D D-TWIN

    • Continuation process of 7D D-TWIN model with Environmental Performance dimension to achieve the holistic D-TWIN Model (8D)

    PREDICTIVE MAINTENANCE

    Perform predictive maintenance and complex simulations in risk-free and virtual environment. Thus, optimises processes and performances by synthesising operational data.

    REMOTE MONITORING

    Virtual Tours enable the users to visit facility with a 360 view, thus enabling end users to make informed decisions and remote monitoring & operations.

    STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY MANAGEMENT

    Structural Integrity Management of offshore assets by combining detailed structural simulation models with sensor data and big data analytics.


    IMPROVED WORKFORCE SAFETY

    Detect early safety scenarios including weights being lifted, fatigue prediction exposures, radiation exposure etc. and accordingly develop instantaneous mitigation actions using the digital twin.
