DQMS – AN AUTOMATED SOLUTION FOR PLANT ASSET DATA QUALITY MANAGEMENT


DQMS, a proprietary solution built by Ceone, provides the ability of automatic extraction of Engineering assets data and validation for quality, completeness and integrity of the Information supplied (including the information received from different vendors). DQMS ensures
  • Consistency of the presentation and format of the information
  • Integrity of the value and accuracy of information
  • Information Completeness measuring the existence of project information against the agreed
  • Timeliness i.e. the availability of up-to-date information whenever required on timely basis
MAINTAINING DATA QUALITY AT EACH STAGE OF THE PROCESS, CALLS FOR A SYSTEMATIC APPROACH TO DATA QUALITY MANAGEMENT.

Process Industry needs solution for data, documents and drawings produced in the Engineering projects to achieve and maintain a high level of quality and meet the needs of information users.

Some of the common Industry Challenges include:
  • Managing Large Engineering Data (Different EPC vendor’s Databases)
  • Duplication of Data
  • Consistency in data management across all engineering phases
  • Lot of manual efforts in preparing the required data templates/standards

  • Data Validation on large set of data

Ensuring Plant Asset Data is accurate, consistent, of good quality and ‘fit for use

Our Solution defines, establishes, implements, monitors and controls the collection, storage, processing and submission of Plant Asset Data to the relevant enterprise Application.

Cloud based User Interface designed for the users to parse, load and validate huge amount of data. It also allows users to manually key in data which shall be stored in the data base directly and processed.


KEY FEATURES

  • Complete Data Management
  • Automatic importing huge data objects in a single upload
  • Maintaining Tag naming standards for the various types of equipment
  • Validation of data quality against defined levels
  • Consistency checking as per Engineering standards
  • Detailed reporting

Value Recognition of Quality Asset Data


  • Improved Return on Investment (ROI)
  • Reduced Asset Data Management workflow redundancies
  • Streamlined Asset Data Distribution
  • Smarter and more sophisticated risk management
  • Allows to focus on core competencies and not feel the need to create shadow data
