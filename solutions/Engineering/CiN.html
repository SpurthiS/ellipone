C-IN PROVIDES QUICK AND ERROR-FREE SERVICES IN INSTRUMENTATION

Ceone, provides the automated ability to generate Instrumentation deliverables using C-IN, our proprietary solution. Using the AI based algorithms and approved engineering standards, all the instrumentation deliverables are generated from the AutoCAD P&ID file.

Deliverables include

I/O List
Junction Box Schedule
Cable Schedule
Material Take-Off – MTO (Electric)
Material Take-Off – MTO (Pneumatic)
Instrument Location Plan
Junction Box Layout
Field Wiring Drawings
Cable Block Diagram
Instrument Loop Drawings
Primary & Pneumatic Hook-up Drawings
I/O Configuration Drawings
Installations and Electrical Hook-up Drawings
Cable Routing Plan
Interconnection Drawings
HOW WE WISHED THERE WAS SOLUTION TO ADDRESS INSTRUMENTATION CHALLENGES IN A CLICK OF A BUTTON

Over the last several decades, generating instrumentation deliverables has become far more accurate and versatile, but can be more complex to configure. Some of the challenges include

  • High manual effort
  • Skill gaps and hence high dependence on existing Experts
  • High lead Time
  • Prone to introduction of manual errors

Keeping the project on track requires a small army of highly skilled people, always in short supply, driving the need for greater efficiency and productivity. Fortunately, there are digital tools to meet this challenge and Ceone provides you that facility.

C-IN: A Unique solution for generating Instrumentation deliverables

Limitations in Market Leading Tools

Existing tools in the market require

  • P&IDs prepared in specific proprietary formats.
  • AutoCAD P&IDs cannot be used for database generation

Huge data entry is required while preparing P&IDs or at database building stage

Reports generated in proprietary software, so

  • Outputs not freely editable or User-friendly
  • Multiple users need to have same proprietary s/w

Change management not completely addressed

  • Data entry for P&ID & Instruments are not linked
  • Inter-dependent data are not updated

Expensive and closed, since based on proprietary software


Advantages of C-IN Automation Suite

Inputs directly from simple AutoCAD P&IDs resulting in

  • Minimum Manual Entry
  • No Complex Configurations

Data is automated based on P&ID data ,project documents, Auto-filling of data using domain expertise

Reports are generated in

  • Basic formats such as MS Excel and AutoCAD
  • Easily usable by multiple users simultaneously

Change Management is efficiently addressed since

  • Latest P&IDs can be downloaded anytime
  • Project rules based automatic updating of data

Economical and User-friendly, since this is based on generally used software such as Excel & AutoCAD

Realizing the Benefits and Business Value

  • Faster project execution enabling delivery of Projects on schedule and within budget
  • C-IN ensures real-time collaboration and alignment between engineering disciplines
  • Optimization in delay and error related costs
  • Reduced dependency on experts and thus their expertise relates costs
  • Increased quality in engineering deliverables ensuring a smoother handover process
