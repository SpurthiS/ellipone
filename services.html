Engineering Services

Ceone Infosolutions is providing Engineering services for various clients. We, with our proven track record, have provided quality services, resulting in value addition and highest customer satisfaction.

Our expertise in Engineering Services to various industries including the Oil & Gas, Power, Energy etc.
along with strong domain knowledge provides a much needed solution for such organisations.

We also provides various engineering services including FEED Engineering, Detail Engineering,As-Built 2D CAD / 3D modelling using SmartPlant, PDMS, PDS, Asset modelling & As Built Updating using Laser scanning i.e. 3D Laser Scanning as well as underground scanning,mapping of assets information for both Greenfield and Brownfield projects

The Engineering professionals will be challenged to acquire new skills and flexibility, and learn new ways of collaborating with Technologies and Solutions.
